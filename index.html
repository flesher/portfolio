About

My name is Matt and I am a problem solver. My passion lies where creativity and technology collide. I am intrigued by intuitive design, fluid interactions, and interesting applications of technology. I am currently a graduate student at Boulder Digital Works in Colorado, where I study interactive design and development. For fun I love to make music, skateboard, ski, and cook.

How I Work…

Plan

The strategy up front is crucial to a successful product. Through research, observation, and analysis I identify exactly what the problem is, who the users are, and what are their pain points. This information sets the stage for design because all decisions will be made according to this data.

  • Competitor Analysis
  • User Interviews
  • Fill in the blank

Design

Armed with the knowledge of the planning phase I begin to concept and design what this product will be. Its an iterative cycle of prototyping, testing, and improving. I like to think outside of the browser as well. There are many amazing technologies emerging in the world of physical computing. Being aware of these allows you to push the product further than you otherwise might.

  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator
  • After Effects

Build

My favorite part of the process is bringing the design to life. I decide what tools are right for the job and go to work coding with clean reusable code. I am constantly reading about and learning new technologies to increase the size of my toolbox for the next job.

  • HTML5 / CSS3
  • Javascript
  • Node.js
