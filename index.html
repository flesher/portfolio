About

My name is Matt and I am a problem solver. My passion lies where creativity and technology intersect. I am intrigued by intuitive design, fluid interactions, and interesting applications of technology. I am currently a graduate student at Boulder Digital Works in Colorado, where I study interactive design and development. For fun I love to make music, skateboard, ski, and cook.

How I Work…

Plan

The strategy up front is crucial to a successful product. Through research, observation, and analysis I identify exactly what the problem is, who the users are, and what are their pain points. This information sets the stage for design because all decisions will be made according to this knowledge.

Design

Armed with the knowledge of the planning phase I begin to concept and design what this product will be. To me, design must come from a human centered approach. I put myself in the user's shoes and figure out how to address their needs in the most effective way possible. Its an iterative cycle of prototyping, testing, and improving.

Build

My favorite part of the process is bringing the design to life. I decide what tools are right for the job and begin coding. I am constantly on the hunt for new technologies. It is astounding what is possible today on the web and in the world of physical computing. This knowledge ultimately translates back to the design process for the next project.
